Soda-Eval measures how well instruction-tuned LLMs reproduce human judgments across coherence, relevance, and style for open-domain conversations. The paper introduces a curated evaluation suite, unified prompts, and contrastive scoring strategies that outperform prior metrics on multilingual testbeds.

Recommended citation: John Mendonca; Isabel Trancoso; Alon Lavie. (2024). "Soda-Eval: Open-Domain Dialogue Evaluation in the age of LLMs." Findings EMNLP 2024.
